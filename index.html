Company Overview

Bridging Supply Gaps.

Streams Energy Resources Limited is a 100% indigenous oil and gas services company, established to deliver dependable procurement and technical support services across Nigeria’s energy sector and beyond. Our operations are guided by professionalism, ethical business conduct and a strong belief that doing the right thing from the start creates sustainable growth.

Our core strength lies in strategic oil and gas procurement and supply chain management, supported by installation, fabrication, maintenance and technical project support services. We enhance operational performance through quality-driven processes, vendor reliability and strict compliance with industry standards.
