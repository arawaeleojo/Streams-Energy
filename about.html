Who We Are

Driving procurement excellence across Nigeria's energy and marine sector.

Executive Summary

A Legacy of Excellence.

Streams Energy Resources Limited is an indigenous Nigerian oil and gas procurement and engineering services company delivering cost-effective, compliant and innovative solutions across the energy, marine and construction sectors.

We operate across upstream, midstream and downstream segments of the oil and gas industry. Our operations are structured to ensure efficiency, transparency, accountability and measurable results.

What We Specialize In

  • Procurement of Industrial and Oilfield Materials
  • Supply of Steel and Pipeline Components
  • Installation and Maintenance Services
  • Engineering and Project Coordination
